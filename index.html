About Me

Michelle is a Certified Scrum Product Owner interested in innovative product design, working with cross-functional teams that foster connections between business goals and technology.

Currently, Michelle is completing her Masters degree focusing her studies in new technologies such as: open access & safe/ethical AI tech. Some of her more recent projects include a published AR Geolocative Audio Mobile application for the University of British Columbia's research team; CEDaR & an AI world-building tech installation that empowers digital creators with AI tools for the non-profit organizations DigiBC and Vancouver Iinternational Film Festival, for their annual Signals 2023 showcase.

In her previous roles, she spent about 3 years as a media strategist, conducting 360 advertising for numerous brands across Canada such as Amazon Prime Video, Keurig Dr Pepper, Puma, Holt Renfrew and more.

With a passion for storytelling and creative, critical thinking, outside of work, Michelle spends her time exploring the outdoors with her camera, perusing new opportunities through education, participating in community-based activities such as community sports, joining social-student councils, as well as occasionally volunteering with local animal shelters.
