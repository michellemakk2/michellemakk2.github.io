About Me

Michelle is a Certified Scrum Product Owner interested in innovative product design, working with cross-functional teams that foster connections between business goals and technology.

Currently, Michelle is completing her Masters degree in Digital Media at the Centre for Digital Media, focusing her studies in new technologies such as: open access & safe/ethical AI tech. Some of her more recent projects include a shipped AR Geolocative Audio Mobile application for the University of British Columbia's research team; CEDaR & an open-AI world-building tech installation that empowers digital creators with AI tools for the non-profit organizations DigiBC and Vancouver Iinternational Film Festival, for their annual Signals 2023 showcase.

In her previous roles, Michelle spent about 3 years as a media strategist, conducting 360 advertising for numerous brands across Canada such as Amazon Prime Video, Keurig Dr Pepper, Puma, Holt Renfrew and more.

With a passion for creative storytelling and critical thinking, outside of work, Michelle spends her time exploring the outdoors with her camera, perusing new opportunities through education, participating in community-based activities such as community sports, joining social-student councils, as well as volunteering with local animal shelters.
